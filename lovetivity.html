Lovetivity

An app for couples — wherever they are — to sweat together, stay accountable, and feel close even when they're far apart.

The problem

Long distance is hard.
Working out alone is harder.

Couples in long distance relationships struggle to stay connected in meaningful, everyday ways. Fitness is something people do alone — but it doesn't have to be. Together makes working out a shared experience, no matter the distance.

Features

What it does.

🤝

Shared workouts

Pick a workout and do it together in real time — same moves, same moment, different cities.

📈

Synced progress

See each other's streaks, milestones, and effort. Stay accountable without the pressure.

💬

Moments of closeness

Reactions, cheers, presence. Small signals that say "I'm here" across the miles.

🌿

No gym required

Built for real life and real schedules. Wherever you are, whatever you have.
