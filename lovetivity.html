The Problem

Long distance is hard.
Staying healthy together is harder.

Most fitness apps are built for individuals. They optimize for your PR, your streak, your goals. But for couples — especially long-distance ones — the motivation to work out is often relational. You do it for each other, and because of each other. Lovetivity is built around that insight: fitness as an act of love, not just self-improvement.

Sources: ¹ Journal of Communication, 2013 · ² Society of Behavioral Medicine, 2012 · ³ Strava internal data, 2022

four core moments.
